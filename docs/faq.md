# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is CampaignBay?

CampaignBay is a comprehensive campaign management platform that automates and optimizes your marketing campaigns. It provides intelligent scheduling, dynamic pricing, and automated campaign execution to maximize your marketing ROI.

### How does CampaignBay differ from other marketing tools?

CampaignBay stands out with its intelligent automation engine, real-time optimization, and seamless integration capabilities. Unlike traditional tools, it automatically adjusts campaigns based on performance data and market conditions.

### Is CampaignBay suitable for small businesses?

Yes! CampaignBay is designed to scale with your business. Small businesses can start with basic campaigns and gradually add advanced features as they grow.

## Campaign Management

### How many campaigns can I run simultaneously?

There's no limit to the number of campaigns you can run. The system is designed to handle multiple campaigns across different channels and product categories.

### Can I pause or modify campaigns after they start?

Yes, you can pause, modify, or stop campaigns at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you can resume paused campaigns when ready.

### What happens if a campaign doesn't perform well?

CampaignBay automatically monitors performance and can adjust campaigns in real-time. You'll receive alerts for underperforming campaigns and suggestions for optimization.

## Pricing and Discounts

### How do I set the right discount amount?

Start with industry benchmarks and test different amounts. CampaignBay's A/B testing feature helps you find the optimal discount level for your products and audience.

### Can I set different discounts for different customer groups?

Absolutely! You can create tiered pricing based on customer segments, purchase history, or loyalty levels.

### What if I want to offer free shipping instead of a discount?

CampaignBay supports various promotional types including free shipping, buy-one-get-one offers, and bonus items. You can mix and match different promotional strategies.

## Automation and Scheduling

### How does the smart scheduling work?

The system analyzes your historical data, customer behavior patterns, and market conditions to recommend optimal campaign timing. It considers factors like peak shopping hours, seasonal trends, and inventory levels.

### Can I set up recurring campaigns?

Yes, you can create recurring campaigns for regular promotions like weekly sales, monthly specials, or seasonal events.

### What triggers can I use for automated campaigns?

Common triggers include inventory changes, customer actions, time-based events, and external factors like competitor pricing or market conditions.
